Welcome to our Minsk Zoo website!

Minsk Zoo is a unique museum of wildlife. It was opened on August 9, 1984. The collection of animals includes more than 400 species of exotic animals and rare representatives of the fauna of Belarus. Waterfowl live all year round in the natural Svisloch backwaters; domestic lambs and goats, which can be stroked, walk on the Vyaskovaya Syadziba contact area. Permanent exhibitions are open, attractions, cafes, horse riding or ponies work. A visit to the zoo is a wonderful vacation and communication with nature.

European Bizon (Zubr)

Zubrs (bisons) are one of the major things you would enjoy at the Minsk Zoo! You can feed them, pet them and take wonderful selfies in front of them!

Terrarrium

More than 70 species of reptiles (snakes, lizards, aquatic and land turtles, crocodiles), amphibians, insects and arachnids. You can see Indian and black-necked spitting cobra, the largest snake in the CIS - a net python named Cleopatra, milk snakes, a decorated slingshot, a chameleon, huge cockroaches, tarantulas.

African Animals

White tiger, giraffe, lions and more african animals you can see at our Zoo! Definitely, you won't be able to play with them but there are shows wher we show how to feed them.

Activities

The environmental activities of the Minsk Zoo include:
  • - conservation and breeding of wild animals in the zoo;
  • -conducting observations to identify favorable conditions for the life of animals;
  • -creation for animals of the zoo of an optimal habitat, taking into account the concept of enrichment of living conditions;
  • -enlightenment of the population in environmental issues;
  • - informing the public about the environmental problems and environmental activities of the zoo;
  • -cooperation with environmental organizations, environmental centers, educational institutions, youth groups;
  • -ecological, aesthetic and spiritual education of the younger generation.
